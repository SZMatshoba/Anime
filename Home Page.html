Anime

What is Anime?

Anime is a Japenese animated entertainment that has become increasingly popular around the world in recent years.
While anime is deeply rooted in Japan, it has since spread to other countries. Anime has a long history in Japan dating
back to the early 20th century, the first known anime was released in 1917.

What makes anime special?

The community is one of the things that make anime so great. The community is a vast collection of people who have
a deep passion for anime and is always willing to accept new people. The community is so welcoming to newcomers
that people are always willing to assist in giving recommendations in what to start watching and the different websites to
watch it from. Since anime is an animated series there is no real time limit to the shows, every season several dozen
new shows get released to the internet.
